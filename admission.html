Admission Process

Follow these steps to get your children enrolled at Brainer Pre School

1

Contact

Call [] or [] for any enquiries or you send us an email to admin@brainerschool.co.tz and we assist you with any information you require to get your child enrolled

2

School Visit

Once the application is received, a meeting will be setup with the school administration. During this meeting the school will access which class may be the right fit for your child.

3

Application

Download application form, fill it out and send it to admin@brainerschool.co.tz If you need any assistance filling the form you can call us or send an email.
